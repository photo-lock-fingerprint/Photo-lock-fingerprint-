Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Photo Vault ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our Photo Vault mobile application.

Core Principle: Photo Vault is designed with privacy as the fundamental principle. The app works completely offline and collects zero data.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be absolutely clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifiable information
  • No Photo Access: We don't access, view, analyze, or upload your photos
  • No Internet Connection: The app has no internet permission and cannot transmit any data
  • No Analytics: We don't collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any analytics
  • No Location Data: We don't collect GPS or location information
  • No Contacts: We don't access your contact list or social connections
  • No Device Information: We don't collect device IDs, IMEI numbers, or hardware information

3. How Your Photos Are Handled

3.1 Storage Location

All photos are stored locally on your device only in the app's private directory. Photos are never uploaded to:

  • Our servers (we don't have any)
  • Cloud storage services
  • Third-party servers
  • Any external storage

3.2 Security Measures

  • PIN Protection: Your PIN is hashed using SHA-256 algorithm before storage
  • Biometric Security: Uses Android's built-in Biometric API (we don't handle fingerprint/face data)
  • Android Sandbox: Photos stored in Android's sandboxed storage, protected by the operating system
  • No Network Access: The app has no internet permission, preventing any data transmission

3.3 Photo Deletion

When you delete photos:

  • Photos are permanently deleted from device storage
  • No copies are retained anywhere
  • No recovery is possible through the app
  • Standard Android file recovery tools might recover deleted files until overwritten

4. Permissions Explained

Photo Vault uses minimal permissions:

  • No Internet Permission: The app cannot connect to the internet
  • Biometric Permission: Only for fingerprint/face unlock (handled by Android OS)
  • No Gallery Permission: Uses Android's Photo Picker API instead (doesn't need access to all photos)

5. Third-Party Services

Photo Vault does NOT use any third-party services:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No cloud storage providers
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment processors (app is free)

6. Children's Privacy

Photo Vault is safe for all ages because:

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • No internet connectivity means no data collection
  • No age verification needed
  • No targeted content or advertising
  • Compliant with children's privacy regulations (COPPA)

7. Security

We implement these security measures:

  • Photos stored in Android's sandboxed private storage
  • PIN protection with SHA-256 hashing
  • Biometric authentication using Android's secure APIs
  • No network connectivity prevents remote access
  • Photos encrypted at the device level

Important: While we implement security measures, no system is 100% secure. You should:

  • Keep your device secure
  • Remember your PIN (it cannot be recovered)
  • Back up photos regularly
  • Keep your Android OS updated

8. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Photos: Remain on your device until you delete them
  • App Data: All app data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • PIN: Hashed PIN is stored locally; forgotten PIN requires app reinstallation
  • No Server Data: We don't have servers, so no data is retained on our end

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data:

  • Right to Access: No data exists to access
  • Right to Deletion: All data is on your device; delete within the app
  • Right to Correction: Not applicable as no data is collected
  • Right to Portability: Not applicable as no data is collected

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date
  • Significant changes will be noted in app updates
  • Continued use of the app means acceptance of changes

Summary: Photo Vault doesn't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your photos remain on your device, protected by PIN/fingerprint, and never leave your possession.
