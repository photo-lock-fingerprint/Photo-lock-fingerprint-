About Photo Vault

Our Mission

At Photo Vault, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right, not a luxury. In today's digital world where personal data is constantly collected, tracked, and monetized, we offer a sanctuary for your private memories.

Our mission is simple: Provide everyone with secure, private photo storage that respects your privacy and gives you complete control over your personal photos.

Why We Created Photo Vault

We created Photo Vault out of frustration with existing "secure" photo apps that:

  • Require internet permissions
  • Collect user data
  • Show intrusive ads
  • Store photos on their servers
  • Charge monthly subscriptions for basic privacy

We asked: "What if a photo vault actually respected privacy?" The answer became Photo Vault.

Our Core Principles

1. Privacy First

Every feature in Photo Vault is designed with privacy as the core principle. We don't collect data because we don't need to. The app works perfectly offline.

2. Complete Transparency

We're clear about what we do (and don't do):

  • We don't have access to your photos
  • We don't collect analytics
  • We don't show ads
  • We don't charge money

3. Security Without Compromise

  • PIN protection with SHA-256 hashing
  • Biometric authentication using Android's secure APIs
  • Local storage only - no cloud backups
  • No internet connectivity = no data leaks

4. Free Forever

Privacy shouldn't cost money. Photo Vault is completely free with no:

  • Hidden charges
  • In-app purchases
  • Subscription fees
  • Feature limitations

How Photo Vault Works

For Users:

  1. Set Your PIN: Create a secure 6-digit PIN
  2. Add Photos: Select photos using Android's secure Photo Picker
  3. Photos Are Secured: Originals removed from public gallery
  4. Access Anytime: Unlock with PIN or fingerprint
  5. Recover When Needed: Restore photos to gallery with one tap

Technical Architecture:

  • Platform: Native Android (Kotlin/Java)
  • Security: SHA-256 hashing, Android Biometric API
  • Storage: Local device storage only
  • Permissions: Minimal - no internet, no gallery access
  • Design: Material Design 3 compliant
